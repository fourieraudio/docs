## Auto-Renewal
Every year, a new transform.suite edition will be released.

When you purchase the transfom.suite '26, your purchase will be set to auto-renew in 365 days to the transform.suite '27.

You will receive multiple emails in advance of this auto-renewal, with details of the transform.suite 2027 price and plugins.

If at any point prior to your auto-renewal you wish to not auto-renew, you can opt out in the ‘Subscriptions’ pane of your account.

## Early Upgrade

If you wish to upgrade to the transform.suite ’27 prior to the expiry of your transform.suite ’26 licence, you will be offered a pro-rata credit.

eg. If you buy transform.suite ’26 on 1st July 2026, and then choose to upgrade to transform.suite ’27 on 1st January 2027, you will be given a credit of 181/365 of your transform.suite ’26 purchase.
